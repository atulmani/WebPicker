About Us

FOUNDER: Nidhi Sahay

Crop Queens is an initiative of CSDSE Private Limited . We at crop queens are closely associated with farmers and agriculture sector & food industry. This endeavor is our dream to change the scenario in agriculture and Food Processing to promote. This portal is dedicated to the growth of farmers and farming and food processing in our country. CSDSE has a team of competent professionals with rich experience as per industry standards.

Main objective of CropQueens.com is to streamline the agriculture sector with process oriented and simple platform that can be easily used by end user. We plan to put Indian farmers in global scenario and integrate Indian farming with global farming. Also, farmer should be benefited in terms of financial gain. Indian Agriculture sector needs reform in terms of modern age farming technologies. Crop Queens is bridging the gap between Indian agriculture and food processing industries.
