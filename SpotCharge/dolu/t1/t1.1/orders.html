TPLiVE

TPLiVE is a startup whose aspiration is to join hands with Digital India and Go-Green. Our mission is to automate the process of creating any kind of events like Educational, Sports & Fitness, Art, Music, Dance & Drama, Cultural, Online Shopping and many more... Organizing any Tournament manually is always a challenge starting from registration forms distribution & collection, payment collection, draws & match schedule preparation & publish, umpire & score management, publish result till prize distribution. Our product aims at eliminating all the challenges faced by the Organizers and the Participants. This Mobile-Enabled Product is designed in an inventive way to ensure uninterrupted and smooth usage. Through this, application users can interact and keep themselves updated about upcoming tournaments, LiVE match updates, results and other Sports related events.



Objective

We will be happy to provide our services to all the Tournament Organizers from District & State Sports Committees, Sports Academies, Schools, Corporates, Townships, Societies and every enthusiastic Sports lover to encourage Sports with health and happiness in their locality. Please reach out to us through whatsapp or email or to meet us at our any upcoming events.
