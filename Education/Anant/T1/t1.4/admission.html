MBBS ADMISSION IN ABROAD

Students can get admission for MBBS in Abroad Medical Universities by following simple steps written below.



STEP - 1

Fill an online form on our Website for a particular University and pay initial documentation fees to get the admission done (This documentation fee will be adjusted in your Processing fees). Send your 10th and 12th mark sheet scanned copy, NEET scorecard, and Passport by email at admission.ishanoverseas@gmail.com (if you don't have a Neet scorecard / or you've just applied for a passport then you can send it afterward).


STEP - 2

Once you complete step 1, you will get your admission letter within 4 working days after the successful submission of your documents and initial payment.
After that, we need your original passport and all the mentioned documents to send for verification(Apostle).
After that University will send your invitation letter and then finally your passport will be sent to the concerned embassy for visa documentation.


STEP - 3

We will let you know when the visa is done, after that tickets will be blocked as per the schedule of the batch flying. We will let you know about the ticket details and all other essentials to carry before your departure. You need to deposit the remaining processing fees before 7 days of your departure.
One of our consultant will accompany you to the university and stay with you until classes will start. Now you you are all set to fly to your dream university for your MBBS Program.



Check Your Eligibility Criteria



The candidates must have passed 10+2 with the subject of Physics, Chemistry, and Biology securing 50 percent marks on each subject from the recognized board


The candidates must have NEET Score Card ( Mandatory )


The candidates must have qualified from the National Entrance cum Eligibility Test.


Candidates age must be more than 17 years.


Applicant is not suffering from any contagious deceases i.e., tuberculosis, HIV AIDS etc.
