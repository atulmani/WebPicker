01 Raise a request

If you would like to access our services, please contact with us using any mode of communication like whatsapp, phone, email or contact form here.





02 Meet Our Experts

As soon as we receive request from you, our experts will contact you to know your requirement in detail accordingly will move forward to next step.





03 Register Your Device

Once requirement has been finalized & access granted , you can register your device with us where you would like to publish your advertisements.





04 Setup Your Campaign

You can access our system to setup your campaigns and configure it as per different schedules accordingly will publish on your mapped devices.

You can customize your advertisement contents like text, images & videos with the help of our experts to improve the quality of the advertisements.





06 Publish On Cloud

Publish your Campaign to the Cloud. The advertisements will execute on the mapped devices as per the schedule configured in our system.
