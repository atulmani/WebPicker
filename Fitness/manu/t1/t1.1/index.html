Personal Gain

Yoga as a Healing Art

Healing is a core element of both yoga philosophy and yoga practice. In fact, healing is something we all aspire to, even if we do not always realize it

Self-unfoldment

We have heard or read that yoga offers us wisdom that will point us in the right direction and shed light on our path, we become seekers of true knowledge.

Strength, Balance & Flexibility.

Improve flexibility is one of the first and most obvious benefits of yoga. During your first class, you probably won’t be able to touch your toes.

Enlightenment

The domain of seekers who have already gone beyond the level of knowing what life is about, who are now acquiring the highest level of realization.

Drains lymphs & boosts immunity

When you contract and stretch muscles, and come in and out of yoga postures, you increase the drainage of lymph (a viscous fluid rich in immune cells).
